The Turks and Caicos Islands (TCI) is one of the most beautiful island in the world, located near the center of the Caribbean. The biggest industry in the TCI is tourism. The tourism industry began in 1980's and grew larger and larger by the years as people began to fall inlove with the beauty of the islands. TCI is truly a tropical paradise. The beaches are covered in pure, white sand with turqouise, glassy oceans and vibrant vegetation. In 2018, the islands recieved the title of having the best beaches in the world. So come and visit the islands so that you can truly see for yourself!
